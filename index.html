Our Mission

We're a data lab modeling human embodied intelligence.

Despite decades of research, we still barely understand human physical, cognitive, or behavioral intelligence. Our goal is to learn how humans interact with the world.

We collect and label video, sensor, audio, and long-horizon data through our 100,000+ contributor network and 500+ industry partners across homes, hotels, restaurants, agriculture, industrial, construction, retail, etc.

Our technology is foundational infrastructure for automating manual labor, increasing global abundance, shifting human effort toward creativity, and advancing how we understand the brain, cognition, prosthetics, and rehabilitation.

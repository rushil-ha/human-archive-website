You ("Contributor") agree to these terms with Human Archive, Inc. ("Human Archive").

1. What you contribute

First-person ("egocentric") video clips of everyday tasks, captured with your device's cameras and motion sensors. Clips contain video and motion data only — Human Archive does not record audio.

2. License you grant

You keep ownership and authorship of your clips. You grant Human Archive a worldwide, perpetual, irrevocable, non-exclusive, royalty-free, fully paid-up, sublicensable license to host, reproduce, adapt, create derivative works from, and use your clips and their metadata to develop, train, evaluate, and improve machine-learning and robotics models, and to include them in datasets Human Archive licenses to its customers for those purposes.

3. Drafts stay on your device

Nothing leaves your device until you tap Submit. Re-record or delete any draft before then.

4. Quality check

After each clip is recorded, your device runs an automated check that your hands are visible and the clip shows real, task-related activity. This check runs entirely on your device; no frames leave your device for it.

5. Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years old to contribute.

6. Deleting your account

You can delete your account anytime in Settings → Account → Delete Account. Human Archive then deletes your account and personal information. Clips you contributed that were approved are de-identified — unlinked from your identity — and kept as part of Human Archive's datasets; clips that were not approved are deleted. We'll tell you what was deleted and what was kept.

7. Privacy

How Human Archive handles your information, including biometric-related data, is described in its Privacy Policy — please read it.

8. Acceptable use

When you record, you agree to the Recording Rules, which govern what you may and may not capture. We review submissions and may reject or remove content, and suspend accounts, for violations.
